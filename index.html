We operates the Location Based Reminder mobile application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect and Use

To provide and improve our Service, we collect several different types of information.

A. Personal Data

When you register for an account using Google Sign-In via Firebase, we collect the following personally identifiable information:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Mobile Number
  • Date of Birth

B. Event and Reminder Data

To fulfill the core functionality of the app, we store data explicitly created by you, including:

  • Event names, messages, and scheduled dates/times.
  • Custom geographic coordinates (latitude and longitude), location names, and radius parameters for location-based alerts.

C. Location Data

We use and store information about your location if you give us permission to do so.

  • Foreground Location: Used to display your current position on the map when selecting a reminder radius.
  • Background Location: Location Based Reminder collects location data to enable geofenced alerts and trigger reminder messages when you enter or leave a specific radius, even when the app is closed or not in use.

You can enable or disable location services when you use our Service at any time, through your device settings.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data for various purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the core features of the Service.
  • To authenticate your account securely using Firebase.
  • To trigger push notifications and alerts based on your specified time and location parameters.
  • To provide customer support.

These third parties have access to your Personal Data only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

  • Google Play Services: Used for core Android functionalities and location services.
  • Google Firebase: Used for secure user authentication (Google Sign-In) and database storage.

4. Data Security

The security of your data is important to us. We utilize industry-standard practices, including Firebase's secure infrastructure, to protect your personal and location data. However, remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We will retain your Personal Data only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. You have the right to request the deletion of your account and associated data. You can delete your data directly within the app's settings menu or by contacting us directly.
